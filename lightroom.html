What do I mean by Color-Grade?

When trying to capture a certain scene, as essential as it is to be aware of:

  • The lighting
  • The Subject
  • The Background
  • The Angle
  • The Composition
  • The Camera Settings
  • The Lense

you can, quite literally, change the entire look and feel of the photo in post.
By enhancing and de-enhancing the exposure and colors of a photo you can
achieve a certain look you have in mind from a movie or a Tv Show
