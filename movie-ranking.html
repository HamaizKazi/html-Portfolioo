The Best Movies/TV-Shows According To Hamaiz

Top Three Movies/Shows You Need To Add To Your Watch-List.

Spider-Man: Into The Spider-Verse

Bitten by a radioactive spider in the subway, Brooklyn teenager Miles Morales
suddenly develops mysterious powers that transform him into the one and only
Spider-Man. When he meets Peter Parker, he soon realizes that there are many
others who share his special, high-flying talents. Miles must now use his
newfound skills to battle the evil Kingpin, a hulking madman who can open
portals to other universes and pull different versions of Spider-Man into our world.

Parasite

Greed and class discrimination threaten the newly formed symbiotic
relationship between the wealthy Park family and the destitute Kim clan.

Breaking Bad

Mild-mannered high school chemistry teacher Walter White thinks his life
can't get much worse. His salary barely makes ends meet, a situation not
likely to improve once his pregnant wife gives birth, and their teenage son
is battling cerebral palsy. But Walter is dumbstruck when he learns he has
terminal cancer. Realizing that his illness probably will ruin his family
financially, Walter makes a desperate bid to earn as much money as he can
in the time he has left by turning an old RV into a meth lab on wheels.
